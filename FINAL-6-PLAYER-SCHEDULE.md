## Why Alice and Bob Play 6 Matches

With 15 partnerships (odd number):
- 15 partnerships ÷ 2 = 7.5 matches
- We create 8 matches to use all partnerships
- Match 8 must reuse one matchup (Eve+Frank vs Alice+Bob)
- Alice and Bob appear in both Match 1 and Match 8

**Trade-off:**
- ✅ **Benefit:** Everyone partners with everyone (complete social mixing)
- ⚠️ **Cost:** Alice and Bob play 1 extra match (6 instead of 5)

This is the **optimal solution** for ensuring complete partnership coverage!
