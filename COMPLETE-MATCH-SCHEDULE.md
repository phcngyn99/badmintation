## Key Features

### 1. Balanced Teams (Matches 2-7)
- Each team has 1 player who just played (tired)
- Each team has 1 player who rested (fresh)
- Fair competitive balance

### 2. Partnership Separation
- Players who just partnered are separated in next match
- Example: Alice+Bob in Match 1 → Alice+Eve and Bob+Frank in Match 2

### 3. Rest Distribution
- No player plays more than 2 consecutive matches
- Regular rest periods for all players
- Fair physical load

### 4. Social Mixing
- Everyone partners with everyone
- Maximum variety in partnerships
- Great for social events

4. **Sustainable:** Players get adequate rest

## Mathematical Note

With 15 partnerships (odd number) and 2 partnerships per match, we can create 7 matches using 14 partnerships. The 15th partnership (Eve+Frank) either:
- Creates an 8th match (with one duplicate matchup), OR
- Remains unused (2 players never partner)

The algorithm chooses to create the 8th match to ensure everyone partners with everyone.

**This is the mathematically optimal solution for 6 players!**
