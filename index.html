🎯 Scoring Rules
Minimum Score Winner must score ≥21 points
Win Margin Must win by 2 points (e.g., 21-19, 22-20)
Maximum Score Game ends at 30 points (no need to win by 2)
Special Rule If winner has 30, loser must have 28 or 29
📊 Score Examples
✅ Valid 21-19, 22-20, 23-21, 24-22, 30-28, 30-29
❌ Invalid 21-20 (not win by 2), 23-12 (not win by 2), 30-27 (loser < 28), 31-29 (max is 30), 21-21 (no ties)
