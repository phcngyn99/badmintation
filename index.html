Scoring Rules

How to Determine Winner
Round-Robin Individual players ranked by: Wins → Win % → Points Scored
Random Pairs Teams ranked by: Wins → Win % → Points Scored
Wins Total matches won (most important)
Win Rate Wins ÷ Total Matches × 100% (tiebreaker)
Points Total points scored (final tiebreaker)
